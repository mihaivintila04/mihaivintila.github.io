Go/No-Go Gauge Design

SOLIDWORKS / CAD Mechanical Design

Project Background

In manufacturing, efficiency and accuracy are critical. A Go/No-Go gauge is a precision inspection tool used on assembly lines to physically verify that a machined part matches exact geometric tolerances without the need for complex, time-consuming measuring tools. If the part fits the "Go" side and fails to fit the "No-Go" side, it passes quality control.

My Role & Methodology

  • Drafted comprehensive 3D solid models of the gauge using SOLIDWORKS.
  • Calculated and applied strict geometric dimensioning and tolerancing (GD&T) specifications to ensure high-precision inspection capabilities.
  • Optimized the physical design for ergonomics and durability on a factory floor.
  • Generated technical drawings and presentation materials to effectively communicate the design intent.
