Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU)

Digital System Logic Hardware Design

Project Background

The Arithmetic Logic Unit (ALU) is one of the fundamental building blocks of a computer's Central Processing Unit (CPU). For this project, I designed an ALU to perform essential arithmetic and logical operations, proving out the core concepts of digital system design and binary mathematics at the hardware level.

My Role & Methodology

  • Engineered the logic gate architecture required to execute mathematical operations (addition, subtraction) and bitwise logical operations (AND, OR, XOR).
  • Programmed and synthesized the design onto a DE10-Lite Intel FPGA board.
  • Tested the hardware implementation using the board's switches and LED displays to verify correct binary outputs.
  • Troubleshot logic timing and signal routing issues to ensure stable performance.
